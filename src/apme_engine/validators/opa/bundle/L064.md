## Avoid end_play (L064)

Avoid `meta: end_play` which stops the entire play for all hosts. Prefer `meta: end_host` to stop only the current host.

Checks for `meta` module with `end_play` in `_raw_params`.

**Violation:** `ansible.builtin.meta: end_play` — **Pass:** `ansible.builtin.meta: end_host`
